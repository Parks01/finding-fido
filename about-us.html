OUR MISSION

Finding Fido is an online resource for finding your pup mate from a local Seattle shelter. We connect you to the available dogs in the Seattle area for an easier search process.

FINDING FIDO strives to help as many dogs find a loving family and forever home.

Our organization was founded on the belief that animals are entitled to kind and respectful treatment at the hands of humans and must be protected under the law.

We Believe- Helping vulnerable animals and keeping pets in safe and loving homes requires a commitment from all of us—advocates, pet owners, shelters, leaders, and entire communities. When we work together under a common cause, we’re both saving lives and elevating our society and its laws to ensure cruelty victims and other at-risk animals receive the protection and care they deserve.
